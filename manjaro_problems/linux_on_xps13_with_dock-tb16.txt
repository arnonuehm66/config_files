Disable Secure Boot:
---------------------

 - Boot and hit F12 for one time boot menu.
 - Select disable secure boot, confirm as often as you must.



Enter BIOS:
------------

 - Disable Intel RAID, to see internal NVMe M2 module.
 - Disable Thunderbolt security, for enabling TB16 dock support.
